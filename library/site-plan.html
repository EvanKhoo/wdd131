Khoo Family Library – Site Plan

Evan Khoo

WDD 131-01

Overview

Purpose

To get my library out into the world and create a catalogue for people to view and consider the great books of the world.

Audience

Readers and book enthusiasts looking for an updated catalogue of classical literature for their viewing pleasure.

Dynamic elements

When a user clicks on a book, a modal will open and provide a brief description, a recommendation for the type of person who would enjoy the book, and a reading level suggestion. The book catalogue will be pulled in dynamically using JavaScript. The webpage will also adjust responsively depending on device size.

Content

Home page

The home page will introduce the Khoo Family Library, highlight featured classical books, and provide a visually appealing introduction to the collection. It will include the logo, navigation, and a brief explanation of what the digital library offers.

Catalogue Page

The catalogue page will list all books dynamically. Clicking a book will open a modal containing a description, recommended audience, and reading level.

Wireframes

Create two wireframes for your site. One for each page and list them here.

Home
